Why join the Challenge?

If you want to reduce your carbon footprint, you need to understand it. And to understand it, you need to measure it. Meter reading is a great first step.

The second step is to start making changes that actually reduce energy consumption, which requires the knowledge of what to change and the motivation to do it.

By pooling our experience and sharing the effort, The Energy Challenge aims to help give you that knowledge and motivation.

Who's doing this?

The North London Energy Transition is made up of members drawn from local Transition groups, including Transition Dartmouth Park, Transition Highgate, Transition Kentish Town and Transition Tufnell Park.
