Why join the Challenge?

Because it is much easier to reduce energy consumption if we do it together.

Why get an energy meter?

Because energy meters provide feedback on consumption, and knowing how much energy you use is the first step to reducing it (but if you don't want a meter, you can still join the Challenge if you don't mind taking meter readings manually).

Why get the Owl meter?

The Owl Intuition-e electricity meter is the only one we know of that will (soon) allow data to be shared online, so that we will eventually be able to share and compare our meter readings automatically (at least that's the plan!).

Who's doing this?

The North London Energy Transition is made up of members drawn from local Transition groups, including Transition Dartmouth Park, Transition Kentish Town and Transition Tufnell Park.
